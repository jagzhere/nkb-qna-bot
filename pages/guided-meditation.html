Guided Meditation

Ram Ram Practice with Maharaj-ji

Follow along with this guided Ram Ram meditation practice. Let Maharaj-ji's love fill your heart as you repeat the sacred mantra.

How to Practice

  1. Find a quiet, comfortable place where you won't be disturbed
  2. Sit comfortably with your spine straight but not rigid
  3. Close your eyes gently or soften your gaze downward
  4. Begin breathing naturally and allow your body to relax
  5. Start the guided meditation and follow along with the voice
  6. When your mind wanders, gently return to "Ram Ram"
  7. End with a moment of gratitude to Maharaj-ji

Practice Tips

  • Start with just 1 minute if you're new to meditation
  • Consistency is more important than duration - practice daily
  • Don't worry if your mind is busy - this is normal
  • Let the mantra "Ram Ram" arise naturally from your heart
  • Practice at the same time each day to build a routine
  • Remember that Maharaj-ji is always with you during practice
